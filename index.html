About Us

We are student church bell-ringers from the University of Nottingham and Nottingham Trent University, but we welcome locals of university age too! Our members range from absolute beginners, to those who have been ringing for years.

Our regular practice is every Tuesday (except outside of term time) from 7PM to 8:30PM at All Saints’ Church, Radford. We also ring for Sunday Services from 10AM to 10:30AM. Though naturally, the pub is where you’re most likely to find us!

You can contact us on Facebook or email us at master@nuscr.org!
