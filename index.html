About Us

We are student church bell-ringers from the University of Nottingham and Nottingham Trent University, but we welcome locals of university age too! Our members range from absolute beginners, to those who have been ringing for years.

Our regular practice is every Tuesday from 7PM until 8:30PM at All Saints’ Church, Radford. We also ring for Sunday Services there from 10AM until 10:30AM. We don’t ring outside of University of Nottingham term time.
